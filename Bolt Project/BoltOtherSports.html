Other Sports

Bolt has always been interested in cricket and soccer, as he has played them since his early childhood. He may play for an Australian cricket team after his running career: the manager has said that there is a spot open for him if he would like to join. He also has a good chance of making it onto Manchester United, which is arguably one of the world's best soccer teams.
